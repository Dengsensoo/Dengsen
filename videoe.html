As the title is, these are videos of the countries I traveled to.
There are so many reasons to travel around the world, and the common reason is to get to know those countries.

My choice is to stay in a country for at least a month. It feels more like living around the world than traveling around the world.
As such, I was able to feel the atmosphere of each country deeply, and I had many interesting and precious experiences that were difficult to explain in words.
When I moved to another country, I felt very strange and thought a lot about various things.

Starting in March 2023, I post the major videos I'm living in Georgia as of August 2024.
Unlike the videos of travel YouTubers, my videos are not put a lot of effort into editing.
